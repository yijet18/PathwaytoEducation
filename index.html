    Education

    Education is the process of gaining knowledge, skills, values, and understanding that help people grow and improve their lives. It occurs in schools, homes, and communities through both formal teaching and everyday experiences. Education enables individuals to think critically, make informed decisions, communicate effectively, and develop good character. It also prepares people for jobs, strengthens societies, reduces poverty, and promotes equality. Overall, education is a powerful tool that shapes individuals and helps build a better, more developed world.

    Education in Malaysia

    Malaysia’s education system is divided into early childhood, primary, secondary, post-secondary, tertiary, and lifelong learning. It is overseen mainly by the Ministry of Education (MOE) and the Ministry of Higher Education (MOHE).
